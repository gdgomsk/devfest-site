Get around Omsk

From other City

You can arrive by plane or by train. Both airport and railway station are inside the city border. So venue is easily reachable by car, public transport or taxi.

Public Transit

Google Transit is available in Omsk. So you can find your way in Google Maps. Moreover, you can download any of the offline public transit apps available for Omsk.

Hotels

Omsk has lots of great hotels and hostels. We suggest you three options: Mayak Hotel, Razumovsky Business Club Hotel and Like Hostel

Hello, Android Wear!

Language: Complexity: Beginner

Take a developer's tour of the Android Wear SDK. Learn how design and create first-class apps for Android Wear. After the session you will be familar with core concepts of wearable computing and be able to create your own app for Android Wear.

Life in Material Era

Language: Complexity: Beginner

Materialize your apps! We will talk about a major change in the design of the year — Material Design. Why it concerns both designers and developers, and how to work with it. After the session you will know how to port your holo apps to material style, as well as how to apply the new paradigm in the design of web interfaces.

Cardboard Reality

Language: Complexity: Beginner

Want a virtual reality headset, but can't afford the hefty pricetag on most existing models? Then Cardboard by Google is your way to virtual reality. It's not exactly the Oculus Rift, but who cares what the outside looks like if the virtual reality experience is as good? After the session, you will know a lot more about features of Google Cardboard, the app and toolkit that let Android users transform their phones into complete AR/VR headsets.

Web Components and Polymer

Language: Complexity: Beginner

Polymer is a new Google technology that allows us to use Web Components in modern browsers. With Polymer we can easily create interactive and maintainable applications using ready components. During the talk you will learn what is Polymer and how to create fast prototypes and applications with material design using it.
